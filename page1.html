A Story of Nanjing Metro

Lynn Zhang

Timeline

In 1984, 20 people's congress deputies from Xuanwu District of Nanjing submitted the proposal of "the idea of Nanjing Metro construction", and the planning of Nanjing Metro project started.
In April 1999, Nanjing Metro was officially approved by the state
On May 28, 1999, Nanjing Metro Corporation was officially established
On September 3, 2005, line 1 was put into operation
On May 28, 2010, line 2 was put into operation
On July 1, 2014, line 10 and line S1 were put into operation
On August 1, 2014, line S8 was put into operation
On April 1, 2015, line 3 was put into operation
On January 18, 2017, line 4 was put into operation
On December 6, 2017, line S3 was put into operation
On December 30, 2017, line S9 was put into operation
On May 26, 2018, line S7 was put into operation 

Three of my favorite Nanjing subway stations

Zhujiang Road Station

Line 1 Zhujiang Road is called candy station by children. The name of candy station is related to Zhujiang Road station adjacent to children's Hospital (from entrance 1, walk west about 200 meters to Nanjing Children's Hospital). It originated from a true story: a couple from Ma'anshan took their sick children to Nanjing Children's hospital beside the station for treatment, and then took the subway back. At Zhujiang Road subway station, the sick child cried and said that he wanted to eat sugar, which made it difficult for the parents who were short of money. Seeing this scene, a subway employee took candy from his pocket for his son and sent it to the sick child. He told the child to listen to his parents and recover as soon as possible. This moved the parents who were sad. Since then, all the staff of Zhujiang Road subway station have brought candy to work.

Xinjiekou  station

Xinjiekou station, known as "the first business district in China", has 24 exits and is the largest subway station in Asia.  Its business concentration is higher than that of Wangfujing in Beijing and Xujiahui in Shanghai, and is the highest in China. Xinjiekou has Deji square, which is huge and high-end, Dayang department store, Suning Xinjiekou store, which is called "the first store in China" by Suning, and Nanjing Xinbai, the first listed company in Nanjing; There are also 11 Starbucks, the first imported product image store of Siemens and the global flagship store of Lv.

 Muxuyuan Station

This is a love station, located in Muxuyuan Station. When waiting for the bus, the head is the Milky way, and the cowherd carries the child to meet with Zhinu. This is the biggest stop of the theme design, the whole picture is 700 square meters, all located on the roof. In order to make the subway passengers have a better sense of positioning, the mural uses a warm and cold contrast technique in the creation of color. Niulang uses warm color to show that he brings warmth to Zhinu. Zhinu uses cool colors to show her situation of being beaten into the cold palace by the queen mother. In order to show the concept of magpie bridge, the sun and moon are used in the middle, and 12 traditional jade patterns are used in the periphery, reflecting the concept of meeting once a year. Disabled elevator incarnation became "magpie bridge", let  Niulang and Zhinu to meet.

The story of a subway driver

Chen Hantao‘s Story

Chen's Daily Life

Before 7:40 am

The subway day shift started earlier, and Chen Hantao had to go to the attendance room before 7:40 every time to prepare for the shift.
After copying the notes of attendance and submitting them to the leader for signature, the preparation work is basically completed. 

Start to work

With everything in order, Chen set foot on the road to the train handover point in good condition.
Arriving at the train handover site, Chen Hantao is waiting for the main line handover with the off duty driver to start the day's safe driving task.
After picking up the train, Chen will first go to the door of the train to check the boarding situation of passengers to ensure everyone's safety. 

Rest Time

For ordinary people, it's normal to eat and use the toilet, but it's not so convenient for subway drivers. They only have a 20 minute break every few hours. At 11:40 noon, Chen Hantao had a meal in the transfer room of hedingqiao station. Inside and outside the room were full of subway workers who had time to rest and eat.
